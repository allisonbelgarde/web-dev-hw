How To Make A Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Ingredients Needed
2 slices of bread
Peanut butter (any brand)
Jelly (any brand, any flavor)
Plate
Butter Knife
IT'S PEANUT BUTTER JELLY TIME, PEANUT BUTTER JELLY TIME -My mother, literally every time she makes said sandwich

Who doesn't love a piece of classic American cuisine? My childhood was filled with delicious peanut butter and jelly sandwiches.
It is a quick and simple snack that is always tasty as well. In this instructable, I will show you how to make this staple of American childhood.

Step 1

  • When you have gathered the ingredients for your PB&J, put one slice of bread on the plate.
  • Use the butter knife to spread the peanut butter on the bread.
  • While noted in the ingredients that you can use any brand, to have the true impoverished American experience, use Great Value brand peanut butter.
  • It is important to use enough peanut butter to cover edge to edge of the bread.

Step 2

  • Next, put the other piece of bread on the plate.
  • Use the butter knife to spread the jelly on the bread.
  • Concerning brand, Welch's is the classic one to use.
  • Concerning flavor of the jelly, grape is the classic one to use.
  • As with the peanut butter, it is important to use enough jelly to cover edge to edge of the bread.

Step 3

  • Put both pieces of bread together, with peanut butter and jelly facing each other.
  • Place the sandwich on the plate.
  • Cut the sandwich diagonally with the butter knife.

And Finally, Enjoy!

Now enjoy a delicious yet simple sandwich. To fully enjoy the experience, drink with a glass of milk.
