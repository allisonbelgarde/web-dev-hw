About the Author

I chose to give instructions on how to make peanut butter sandwiches because it is something I know how to do very well and it truly is and has been a staple of my life. Without being too much of a sob story, my family was very poor when I was a child and we often ate peanut butter and jelly for meals. Even as our situation improved, I would return to peanut butter and jelly for its simplicity and tastiness. For being a poor man's food, it is rather delicious.
